Universe Warriors

Universe 7

Team Universe 7 is a team presented by Beerus, Shin and Whis with the strongest warriors in Universe 7 in order to participate in the Tournament of Power. The team consists of Goku, Vegeta, Piccolo, with Beerus and Whis as the team's supervisors. Later on,Team Universe 7 is expanded to 10 members, adding Gohan, Krillin, Master Roshi, Tien Shinhan, Android 18, and Android 17. Since Majin Buu falls asleep before the tournament, Goku rushes in order to get Frieza as the tenth member of the team.The team was organized to represent Universe 7. Goku and Vegeta are the team's first members due to them being the strongest warriors Beerus has encountered and Beerus instructs them to recruit two of their friends to participate, leaving the last spot for a member for Beerus to choose.


Universe 11

The team consists of Top, Jiren, Dyspo with Belmod and Marcarita as the team's supervisors. Top and Jiren are the team's first members due to them being the strongest warriors Belmod has encountered and Belmod instructs them to recruit two of their friends to participate, leaving the last spot for a member for Belmod to choose.

Universe 6
